About Broadway

Broadway is the beating heart of musical theatre — a world where stories, music, and movement unite on stage to create magic. Located in New York City’s Theatre District, it’s home to more than 40 world-class theatres that have shaped generations of art and culture.

From powerful performances to timeless music, Broadway has inspired millions, producing legends and unforgettable shows that continue to move audiences worldwide.
